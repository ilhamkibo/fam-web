Who Are We?

About Us

We take pride in being pioneers in the creative plastic recycling industry, where our vision embraces sustainability and beauty in one powerful movement. Since our establishment in 2021, EcoFusion has shown that plastic waste can be transformed into high-value artworks that carry functional utility and captivating aesthetics.

Vision

Transformation Through Recycling.

EcoFusion is crystal clear: to reshape the perception of plastic waste. We take hard-to-decompose plastic and grant it a new lease on life as elegant furniture, useful merchandise, functional household tools, as well as captivating adornments and decorations. In every creation of ours, there is a narrative of collective efforts to minimize environmental impact.

Mission

From vision to mission:
  • Creative Recycling and Sustainable Innovation
  • Raising Awareness
  • Elevating Industry Standards
  • Build strong local communities & partnerships
  • Educate future generations
